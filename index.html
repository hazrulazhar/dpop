About a.i.painter

With the COVID-19 pandemic causing art galleries to close indefinitely, the public have no access to experience art. What if we could get the online public to make art inspired by historical artists? a.i.painter lets users explore art by taking or uploading a photo and selecting a masterpiece filter like on instagram and let the A.I. paint the photo. Users will be able to explore works done by others, and learn about the artist behind the original work.
