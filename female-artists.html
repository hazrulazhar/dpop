#StopGenderPayGap

Discrimination Faced

Women in Europe have been fighting discrimination for centuries. We would think the art world would be a safe haven of gender parity, but statistics show this is not true. In the report "The Art Market 2019" by Claire McAndrew produced by the Art Basel and UBS, women's artwork sell for a huge discount compared to men's. Only 2 women have ever broke the top 100 auction sales despite the fact that they are the subject matter for half of the top 25%. This curation was designed to put women's work front and centre and showcase that their work deserves better too.
